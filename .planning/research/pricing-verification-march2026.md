### DDR5 Market Context

Per [Tom's Hardware RAM Price Index](https://www.tomshardware.com/pc-components/ram/ram-price-index-2026-lowest-price-on-ddr5-and-ddr4-memory-of-all-capacities) and [Resell Calendar](https://resellcalendar.com/news/news/ram-price-december-2025-ai-spike-reseller/): AI-driven DRAM demand has caused a severe pricing crisis. 32GB DDR5-6000 kits that were $60-80 in mid-2024 are now $380-$675+. Prices are volatile and stock doesn't last.
